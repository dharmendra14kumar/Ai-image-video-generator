## 🛠️ Using Ai-image-video-generator

### Generating Images Locally

1. Select the **Image Generator** tab in the app.
2. Choose or enter a prompt describing the image you want.
3. Set options for size or style if needed.
4. Click **Generate**.
5. Wait a few seconds while the image is created.
6. Preview the image, and use the **Save** button to store it on your PC.

---

### Creating Videos via API

The video feature relies on an online API. The app guides you through this with simple steps:

1. Select the **Video Generator** tab.
2. Enter your text or video prompt.
3. Choose video length and quality settings.
4. Click **Create Video**.
5. The app sends the request to the API.
6. You will see a progress bar.
7. When done, the video will appear.
8. Save it to your local storage by clicking **Download**.

---

### 4K Upscaling

To improve your image or video resolution:

- Select the file you want to upscale.
- Choose the **4K Upscale** option.
- Click **Start Upscaling**.
- Wait while the program processes your content.
- Your output will have four times the original pixels, offering better clarity.

Upscaling works both for images and videos.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting and Tips

- **App won’t start:** Make sure your graphics card driver is up to date.
- **Slow performance:** Close other heavy programs and check if your GPU is being used.
- **Video generation fails:** Check your internet connection.
- **Images look blurry:** Try changing the prompt or increasing resolution settings.
- **Error messages:** Restart the app. If the problem persists, reinstall the software.
